Privacy Policy

Effective: 1 January 2025 Last updated: 1 March 2026
This policy explains how Clockee collects, uses, and protects the personal information of administrators, staff members, parents, and students within institutions that use the Clockee platform.

1. Who We Are

Clockee is a workforce and student management platform designed for schools and organisations ("Institutions"). References to "Clockee", "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to the operator of the Clockee service. Each Institution that subscribes to Clockee acts as an independent data controller for the personal data of its users.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When an Institution creates accounts on behalf of its users, we collect:

  • Full name and email address
  • Phone number (optional, for notifications)
  • Role within the Institution (admin, staff, or parent)
  • Profile photograph (optional)
  • Institution affiliation and identifier

2.2 Student Information

Institutions may register student profiles that include:

  • Student name, date of birth, and gender
  • Class or grade assignment
  • A unique attendance passcode or QR identifier
  • Emergency contact information
  • Medical notes relevant to school operations (if provided by the Institution)

2.3 Attendance and Activity Data

  • Check-in and check-out timestamps for staff members
  • Student attendance scan records
  • Geographic location at the time of clock-in (if enabled by the Institution)
  • Device identifiers used to submit attendance records

2.4 Communications

  • Direct messages sent between staff, parents, and administrators within the platform
  • Announcements broadcast by administrators
  • Support tickets submitted to Clockee or to Institution admins

2.5 Technical and Usage Data

  • IP address, browser type, and operating system
  • Pages visited and features used within the portal
  • Error logs and performance diagnostics
  • Session tokens (stored as secure, HTTP-only cookies)

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data. We share information only in these circumstances:

  • Within the Institution: Administrators can view data for their own Institution's users. Staff cannot see other staff's personal data. Parents can only see their own child's records.
  • Service providers: We use third-party processors (hosting, email delivery, monitoring) who are contractually bound to handle data only on our instructions.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Clockee, its users, or the public.
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition, data may be transferred to the successor entity under equivalent protections.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as the Institution's subscription is active. When an account deletion request is processed, personal data is permanently removed within 30 days of the deletion date. Anonymised aggregate statistics (e.g., overall attendance rates) may be retained indefinitely.

Institutions may export all data associated with their account at any time via the Data Portal.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction — ask us to pause processing while a dispute is resolved
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, visit the Data Portal or contact your Institution administrator. Requests are typically fulfilled within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Session Tokens

The Clockee web portals use a single HTTP-only session cookie (dp_token) to maintain your authenticated session. This cookie:

  • Is never accessible to JavaScript on the page
  • Expires after 8 hours of inactivity
  • Is deleted when you log out
  • Is not used for advertising or cross-site tracking

We do not use third-party analytics cookies or advertising trackers.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security controls including TLS encryption in transit, encrypted credentials at rest, role-based access controls, and audit logging. However, no system is completely secure; in the event of a data breach we will notify affected Institutions without undue delay, and no later than required by applicable law.

9. Children's Data

Clockee stores student records on behalf of educational Institutions. Student data is provided and controlled exclusively by the Institution. Clockee does not directly collect data from children, and parents or guardians who wish to access or delete a student's record should contact their Institution's administrator.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated to Institution administrators by email at least 14 days before taking effect.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to submit a data rights request, please use the Data Portal. Alternatively, contact your Institution's administrator who can escalate to Clockee on your behalf.
