2. How Data Is Stored

2.1 Database

All personal data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on infrastructure managed by Clockee. The database server is not publicly accessible; it accepts connections only from Clockee application servers over a private network.

2.2 Encryption

  • In transit: All connections between clients and Clockee servers use TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • At rest: Database volumes are encrypted using AES-256. Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes and are never stored or logged in plaintext.
  • Tokens: JWT access tokens are signed with a secret key stored only in server environment variables, never in the database or source code.

2.3 Backups

Automated encrypted backups are taken daily and retained for 30 days. Backups are stored in a separate geographic region from the primary database. Deleted user data is excluded from backups taken after the deletion is processed; however, it may persist in backups created before the deletion for up to 30 days, after which those backups expire.

3. Access Controls

  • Each user can only access data within their own Institution.
  • Administrators can view staff, parent, and student records for their Institution only.
  • Staff members can view only their own profile and attendance records.
  • Parents can view their own profile and their linked children's attendance.
  • Super-administrators at the platform level have access scoped to operational needs and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • All privileged access actions are written to an audit log.

4. Data Exports

Exports are generated as PDF documents and are available immediately for download. Exports are not stored on Clockee servers after delivery.

5. Account Deletion

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once confirmed, your data cannot be recovered.

5.1 Requesting Deletion

Any user may submit an account deletion request via the Data Portal. The request enters a pending state and is processed within 30 days.

During the pending period your account is deactivated — you cannot log in or use Clockee — but your data is not yet deleted. You may cancel the request within this window to reactivate your account.

5.2 Cancelling a Deletion Request

To cancel a pending deletion, log in to the Data Portal before the deletion date and click "Cancel Request". Your account will be reactivated immediately.

5.3 What Is Deleted

When deletion is executed, the following are permanently removed:

User TypeData Deleted
Admin Profile, credentials, support tickets, direct messages. Note: cannot delete if you are the only admin for an active Institution — reassign the admin role first.
Staff Profile, all attendance records, leave records, direct messages
Parent Profile, notification history, direct messages. Student records are not deleted — they are owned by the Institution.

5.4 What Is Retained After Deletion

  • Anonymised aggregate statistics — e.g., overall attendance rates for the Institution, with no link to a specific user.
  • Legal and financial records — if required by applicable law (e.g., payroll records required by labour regulations), data may be retained in a restricted archive for the minimum legally required period.
  • Backup copies — deleted data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before those backups expire.

5.5 Deletion Timeline

6. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting personal data, Clockee will:

  • Contain and investigate the breach as a priority
  • Notify affected Institution administrators within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
  • Provide a written incident report detailing the nature of the breach, data affected, likely consequences, and remediation steps taken
  • Comply with applicable regulatory notification obligations

7. Contact

For questions about data storage, deletion, or export, please use the Data Portal. For urgent data concerns, contact your Institution administrator who can escalate directly to the Clockee team.

You can download all your personal data or submit a deletion request at any time via the Clockee Data Portal.
